Learning Outcomes

This is web-app should be able to:

Internal Trade
  • Describe the meaning and types of internal trade.
  • Specify the services of wholesalers to manufactures & retailers.
  • Explain the services of retailers to wholesalers/manufacturers and consumers.
  • Explain the form of large scale retailers.

International Business
  • Discuss the meaning and scope of International Business
  • Distinguish between Internal and International Business
  • Enumerate the benefits of International Business
  • Discuss the procedure and documents required for import and export transaction
