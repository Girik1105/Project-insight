Learning Objectives

This is web-app should be able to:
  • Describe the meaning and types of internal trade.
  • Specify the services of wholesalers to manufactures & retailers.
  • Explain the services of retailers to wholesalers/manufacturers and consumers.
  • Explain the form of large scale retailers.

Learning Outcomes

In conclusion you shall:
  • Explain the meaning of commerce and trade.
  • Explain different types of business activities and their classification between industrial and commercial activities.
  • Distinguish between primary, secondary and tertiary industrial activities.
  • Develop awareness about the types of risks that a business is exposed to and to understand their nature and causes.
