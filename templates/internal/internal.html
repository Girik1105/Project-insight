Wholesale Trade
Wholesale Trade

Wholesale trade consists of purchasing and selling goods, generally to retailers, professional users or authorities, or to other wholesalers or intermediaries, in large quantities. Wholesale trade intermediaries put buyers and vendors into contact without being owners of the goods.

Services of Wholesalers

Wholesalers provide various services to manufacturers and retailers. They provide immense help in the distribution of goods and services.

Retail Trade
Retail Trade

Retail Trade involves selling of goods and services to the ultimate consumers. A retailer purchases goods in large quantities from manufacturers, directly or through a wholesaler, and then sells in smaller quantities to consumers.

Services of Retailers

Retailers serve as an important link between the producers and ultimate consumers in the distrbution of goods and services.

Fixed Store Trade
