Retail Trade Video

Retailing is that branch of business which is devoted to the sale of goods and services to the ultimate consumers for their personal and non-business use. The retailer normally buys goods in large quantities from the wholesalers and sells them in small quantities to the ultimate consumers.
This video will help you in understanding how Retailers help in transferring the goods from the hands of the manufacturers or wholesalers to the final consumers or users.
