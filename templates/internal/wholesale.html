Wholesale Trade video

Wholesaling is concerned with the activities of those persons or establishments who buys goods from manufacturers/producers and sell these to retailers and other merchants, and/ or to industrial, institutional and commercial users but who do not sell in significant amount to ultimate consumers.
This video will help you in understanding how Wholesalers serve as an important link between manufacturers and retailers.
