About Us

Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire.

  • InSight was a program founded in June 2021 , in response to the paucity of education created due to the pandemic.
  • The platform aims to bring the retired teachers back into the noble cause of spreading education amongst the underpriviliged.
  • This platform is open to all.Further, the content can be created and shared by students as well , as we at InSight believe creativity should have no boundaries.
